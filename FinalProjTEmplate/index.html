Site Summary

Edlen Ring has some of the most incredible boss design in the history of video games. This site will be taking a closer look at some of the highlights of the game and dive into just what makes them so good. Elden ring is now the 3rd spinoff game from the Dark Souls series that FromSoftware has developed and by far the most ambitous. Coming in at over two times longer than any other game in the series with over twice the amount of total bosses, there is just a lot to talk about.
