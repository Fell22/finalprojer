Margit the Fell Omen

Margit is the first main boss of the game and oh boy is he a tough one. An interesting design choice to make the first boss so hard that you are actually supposed to run away and come back later when you're a bit stronger. With a large arsenult of incredibly unique and learnable attacks, Margit stands above almost every other boss in the game in how fun he is to fight despite how early on he is. Deinitely going down as the best opening boss in any videogame that history has delivered us thus far. He even has a part two fight later on that is even stronger. Truly the boss that keeps on giving, Margit is the perfect opener to an incredible experience of a game.
